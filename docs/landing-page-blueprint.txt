### **Core Design & Typographic Principles**
---
* **Atmospheric Depth**: Using dynamic, blurred backgrounds for a premium, tech-forward feel.
* **Asymmetric Balance**: Creating visual interest with off-center, well-spaced layouts.
* **Minimalist Precision**: Applying a clean, grid-aligned, "less is more" approach reflecting Swiss design sensibilities.
* **Conceptual Serif Typography**: Using a distinguished **Serif font** for `KI-Strategien` and `KI-Transformation` to signal authority, paired with a modern **Sans-Serif** for all other text to ensure clarity and a contemporary feel.

---
### **Global Components: The Conversion Modals**
---
* **Report Download Modal**: A simple form (Name, Email) triggered *only* from the "Report herunterladen" button on the Hero screen. This provides a low-commitment conversion path.
* **Booking Modal**: An integrated Calendly widget for booking the "Free AI Readiness Assessment." This is the primary, high-intent conversion action, triggered by all other main CTAs.
